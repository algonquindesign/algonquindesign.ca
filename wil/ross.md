## Overview

 We’re looking for two motivated and adaptable Graphic Design Placement Students to join our in-house creative team. Our team consists of two Graphic Designers, and one Senior Graphic Designer/Team Lead.  This is a fast-paced design environment — not an agency setting — where you’ll collaborate with internal teams to create and maintain high-quality brand and marketing materials. Our team services design work for the entirety of Ross Video.

Our team works entirely virtually, using Asana and Microsoft Teams to manage projects, share feedback, and stay connected. Students must be comfortable with remote communication and self-directed work.

### Focus Areas

We’re specifically looking for:
-	Design Generalists – strong all-round design and production skills.
-	Web Designers – experience or interest in digital and Figma-based design.  

### What You’ll Do

#### Design Generalist

-	Design and produce creative assets for digital and print, including:
-	Long-format documents, brochures, and internal communications
-	Web banners, social media graphics, and marketing collateral
-	Branded templates, stationery, and internal documents
-	Support digital asset management by organizing and maintaining brand and creative files.
-	Manage assigned projects and tasks independently in Asana.
-	Collaborate remotely with marketing, communications, digital teams and more.

####   Web Designer

-	Work in Figma to create auto-responsive blocks and modules
-	Help update old web pages to new designs
-	Help organize component library 

#### Who You Are

-	A self-starter who can manage time and projects with minimal supervision.
-	Enrolled in a Graphic Design, Visual Communication, or related program.
-	Proficient in Adobe Creative Cloud (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign; Figma and After Effects are assets).
-	Comfortable working and communicating in a fully virtual environment.
-	Detail-oriented, creative, and eager to learn how design operates within a corporate setting.
