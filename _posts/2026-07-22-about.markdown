<p>
	AlgonquinDesign.ca is created and maintained by {{ site.data.sitewide.sitewide-info.program-coordinator }}, faculty in the graphic design program.
</p>
<p>
	We built this site because a program is more than a course listing. It has a culture, a community, and a body of student and alumni work worth showing. Those things are hard to capture in an official catalogue. So we capture them here.
</p>
<p>
	This site reflects the perspective of the faculty who teach in the program. It is not a formal institutional publication, and it does not replace the college’s official resources. For authoritative information on admissions, applications, tuition, and program requirements, we link throughout to the college’s <a href="https://www.algonquincollege.com/mediaanddesign/program/graphic-design/">official program page</a>. Please treat that page as the source of record.
</p>
<p>
	If you have questions about the program or the application process, <a href="mailto:{{ site.data.sitewide.sitewide-info.coordinator-email }}">get in touch</a>. Someone who actually teaches here will answer. That direct line is one of the reasons this site exists.
</p>
